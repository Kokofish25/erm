The Pillar Men

The Pillar Men, composed of Santana, Wamuu, Esidisi, and Kars, are part of the Pillar Men Clan. They are also vulnerable to sunlight and Hamon.

Abilities:

Body Manipulation-They can easily manipulate their body to dislocate it, giving an advantage.

Enhanced Senses and Intelligence-The most intelligent life form on Earth, until it went extinct.

Elemental Fighting Style-They can manipulate elements such as Light, Fire, and Wind.

Ultimate Life Form-Using the Stone Mask with the Red Stone of Aja, they can be transformed to a higher level of life form and gained the following abilities:Sunlight Immunity, Complete Immortality, Manipulate their body to any life form, and they can use Hamon.
