A Truly Bizzare Adventure

Jojo's Bizzare Adventure is a Japanese manga series by Hirohiko Araki. It consists of 8 parts : Phantom Blood, Battle Tendency, Stardust Crusaders, Diamond Is Unbreakable, Vento Aureo(Golden Wind), Stone Ocean, Steel Ball Run, and Jojolion. Part 3 onwards, Araki introduced the concept of Stands, to not make the fight scenes boring with just Hamon. The story follows the Joestar Bloodline, Jonathan Joestar, Joseph Joestar, Jotaro Kujo, Josuke Higashikata, Giorno Giovanna, Jolyne Kujo. On a separate universe (p7,p8), Johnny Joestar, and Josuke Higashikata
